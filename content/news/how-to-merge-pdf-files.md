## Why Merge PDFs in the Browser?

Most online PDF tools require uploading your files to remote servers. This raises privacy concerns, especially for contracts, medical records, or financial documents.

JustUse.me takes a different approach — all processing happens in your browser using pdf-lib. Your files never leave your device.

## Step-by-Step Guide

1. Open [JustUse.me Merge PDF](/tools/merge-pdf)
2. Drag and drop your PDF files into the drop zone
3. Reorder pages if needed by dragging
4. Click Merge and download your combined PDF

## When You Need to Merge PDFs

- Combining assignment pages for a school submission
- Merging scanned pages into one document
- Consolidating multiple contracts into a single file
- Putting together a portfolio from separate files

## Privacy Advantage

Unlike Smallpdf or iLovePDF, JustUse.me processes everything client-side. No server ever sees your documents. This makes it safe for sensitive files like tax returns, medical records, or legal contracts.
